Credible sources you can cite for researching and referencing

The Questia library includes more than 83,000 academic books and more than 14 million journal, magazine, and newspaper articles from more than a thousand of the world's leading publishers. Questia offers quality sources selected by librarians and approved by professors, and many of the resources are peer-reviewed!

The right tools for the job.

Questia provides time-saving research tools, like automatic bibliography creation, highlights, notes, citations and more, all designed with the research process in mind.

  • Highlights
    Highlight and save text in six different colors as you read.
  • Notes
    Add notes for yourself directly to book pages or articles.
  • Bookmarks
    Easily return to any page in any book in our library.
  • Bibliographies
    Automatically generate bibliographies in MLA 8, MLA 7, APA or Chicago format.
  • Citations
    Cite passages, pages or entire articles instantly.
  • Projects
    Everything is saved in custom projects that you create, name and organize.
