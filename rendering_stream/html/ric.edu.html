    Academic Classes
    All classes are now being offered remotely aside from select studio and laboratory classes and some first-year courses. 

    Prospective Student Center (PSC)
    The PSC is open and offering virtual campus visits.

    Campus Events and Gatherings
    Events will be held on a limited basis and in accordance with state protocols. If you have questions, contact CECS at 401-456-8900 or events@ric.edu.

    Intercollegiate Athletics
    All varsity athletics are suspended until Spring 2021 with limited opportunities for student-athletes to practice and participate in competition in accordance with health and safety protocols.
