The lifecycle of innovation.

Every product we use, medical treatment we receive and service we consume has been imagined, created and improved in a continuous, connected cycle of innovation.

Clarivate operates at the heart of that lifecycle. We give our customers the lens of clarity—the insights they need to turn bold ideas into life-changing innovations.

  • Discover.  Find new insights, connecting the dots through the most trusted content and analytics.
  • Protect. Design brands and protect inventions using the world’s leading sources of information and technology.
  • Commercialize. Maximize return on investment and successfully enter new markets using unique services, tools and insights.
