  • Harvey Mudd links coral evolution and climate. Biology professor Cathy McFadden co-publishes study on how changing ocean chemistry has affected the evolution of stony and soft corals.

  • Harvey Mudd dives in. Engineering professor Chris Clark receives an NSF grant to develop new underwater robot tracking technology to monitor marine life in Costa Rica.

Mudd Talks

"Self-Publishing for Fun and (Maybe) Profit" –Dec. 3

Former Muddraker staffer turned serial author Kyle Roesler '89 will look at the history of self-publishing, explain how to get started on the self-publishing journey and describe what to expect once that masterpiece is published. So far, Roesler has self-published six novels and a collection of short fiction.
