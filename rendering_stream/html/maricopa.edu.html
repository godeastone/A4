Transferring to a University is Easy

If you’re working toward a bachelor’s degree, Maricopa Community Colleges strives to make the first part of your path affordable. Our transfer pathways put you on track to your four-year degree with a course-specific layout. At Maricopa Community Colleges, you have choices – what you study, where you attend classes, and what you’ll do when you’re done. Whether you want to transfer to a four-year university or move into an in-demand career, our programs are designed to help you reach your goals.

Get a Head Start

Maintain your momentum. Maricopa Community Colleges helps students finish high school and get an early jump on college with the following programs and opportunities:
Achieving a College Education (ACE)

ACE helps eligible students overcome obstacles and move from high school to college with ease.

Dual / Concurrent Enrollment

Take community college classes before you graduate from high school to get a head start on your college credits.

Hoop of Learning

The Maricopa Hoop of Learning helps Native American students finish high school and successfully transition to college.

Enroll in Four Easy Steps

Get Admitted:

Create a Maricopa Enterprise ID (MEID) account, apply for admission, and submit proof of identification.

Determine Placement:

We utilize four processes to determine college course placement: high school transcripts, ACT score, GED score, and placement test.

Register for Courses:

Seek out academic advising, register for classes, and attend New Student Orientation.

Pay Tuition and Fees:

Payment arrangements are due at the time of registration, and there is a non-refundable registration processing fee.
