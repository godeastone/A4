Spotlight: Dec 3, 2020

Since 1938, the Hobby Shop (opens in new window) has embodied MIT’s “mind and hand” motto. In 2020, it became the main prototyping and manufacturing site for an effort (opens in new window) to develop an open-source, low-cost ventilator, as well as a source of Zoom-based how-to demos.
