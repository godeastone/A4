Membership

For $75 a year, or free for students, you can be a member of one of the world's leading standards development organizations. Members help create and update standards while gaining knowledge, leadership skills, professional networks, and more. Your benefits include a free print or online volume of standards, Standardization News magazine, access to meeting minutes and agendas, and more.
