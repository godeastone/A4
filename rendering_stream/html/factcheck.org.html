Video Doesn’t Show Election Fraud in Georgia

December 2, 2020

Conspiracy theorists falsely claimed that a video of an election worker during the Georgia machine recount revealed fraud in the 2020 election. All it showed was an election worker performing a routine part of the process, according to election officials.

A Misleading Dark Money Attack on Ossoff

December 1, 2020

A Republican TV ad falsely suggests that “liberal megadonors” are spending $1 billion in “dark money” to help Democrat Jon Ossoff in Georgia’s Senate runoff race. That’s how much multiple experts estimate may be spent on all candidates in both Georgia Senate elections for the entire 2020 campaign.

Trump Repeats Baseless, False Claims About the Election

November 30, 2020

In his first interview since Election Day, President Donald Trump recapped baseless, false and misleading claims he has made before of a “rigged” election.

Danish Study Doesn’t Prove Masks Don’t Work Against the Coronavirus

November 25, 2020

Q: Did a recent study in Denmark show that face masks are useless for COVID-19?
A: No. The study found that face masks did not have a large protective effect for wearers — not that masks provide no protection at all or don’t offer benefits to others. 

Ad Links Warnock to ‘Defunding the Police’

November 25, 2020

Trump Misinformation on Georgia Ballot Rejections

November 20, 2020

The percentage of mail-in ballots rejected in Georgia due to signature issues this year was about the same as in the 2016 and 2018 general elections — contrary to a tweet by President Donald Trump.

Paul Misleads on Natural Infection and COVID-19 Vaccines

November 19, 2020

In a tweet, Sen. Rand Paul misleadingly suggested that immunity from “[n]aturally acquired” COVID-19 was better than that from a vaccine. But it’s not known how immunity from the two sources compares — and the entire point of a vaccine is to offer immunity without the risk of getting sick.
