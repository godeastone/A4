Unmatched WordPress Performance

Powerful Google Cloud Platform

Our platform is hosted on the Google Cloud and enjoys the fastest network and the latest modern hardware with SSD persistent storage. Google infrastructure is known for innovation, reliability and speed, which guarantees a great performance for any website.

Unique Caching Options

WordPress instances hosted on SiteGround are amazingly fast. By default, we enable NGINX Direct Delivery for all sites on our servers, which means that certain items, like your images for example, will load faster from the memory of the server. However, the biggest impact on the WordPress loading speed is achieved by using the higher levels of our SuperCacher service and connecting your site with our NGINX-based dynamic cache and Memcached service. (The option is available on our GrowBig and GoGeek plans).

Free SG Optimizer Plugin

We provide a free all-in-one optimization plugin, which is specifically created for top website performance in our hosting environment. The SG Optimizer allows you to use all our unique caching options listed above. Through it you can also easily improve your site speed by setting the optimal PHP version, switching on the HTTPS option and using our automated image optimizations, lazy image loading, GZIP compression, CSS&HTML minifications and more.

Free CloudFlare CDN

We've partnered with Cloudflare to provide a free Content Delivery Network (CDN) service to our customers. The CDN tremendously speeds up your load time for visitors from different geographical locations. In order to speed up the delivery of a page, it caches its content, auto-minifies images, exploits RailGun to speed up uncacheable dynamic content, blocks malicious traffic and even reduces SPAM. The CDN network has 194 locations and is growing so your page will be loaded from the one closest to your visitor!
