NEW: Global Synthetic Drugs Assessment 2020

The Global Synthetic Drugs Assessment 2020 provides an analysis of the global synthetic drugs market and highlights dynamics related to the demand and supply of synthetic drugs in a regional context. Synthetic drugs became purer, cheaper & more accessible over the past 10 years.

Executive Director

The Executive Director of the United Nations Office on Drugs and Crime (UNODC) is
Ms. Ghada Waly of Egypt, who took office on
1 February 2020. She holds the rank of Under-Secretary-General of the United Nations.
