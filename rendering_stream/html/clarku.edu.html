Clark at a Glance

Flexibility is built into our liberal arts curriculum, allowing you to select from compelling courses on diverse topics.

ENT 216
Financial Intelligence
Acquire the knowledge and skills you need to successfully manage your money during and after college — both as an individual and an entrepreneur.
CSCI 245
Mobile Software Development
Practice your programming skills and solve real-world problems by learning how to design and develop mobile apps for a wide variety of uses.
PHIL 104
Ethics and Epidemics: From AIDS to COVID-19
What moral and public policy issues do pandemics pose? We take a deep dive into the global AIDS epidemic and ask: How can we do better with COVID-19?
TA 164
American Musical Theatre
Embark on a journey of personal and artistic growth as you explore the vast art form of musical theatre. Present original work based on classical shows.
CHEM 050
Forensic Science
