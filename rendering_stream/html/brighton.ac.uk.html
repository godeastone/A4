COVID-19 update

If you are a Brighton student or you are thinking about studying here you may have questions or concerns about COVID-19. We can help – call our helpline on 01273 641234, lines are open Monday–Friday, 10am–12pm and 1–3pm.

You can also take a look at our FAQs and check the number of confirmed cases of COVID-19 at the university.

We're now implementing the government's guidance to enable students to travel home before the end of term – book your COVID test now.
