Who is Mike Mann?

Mike is the founder of several successful, active, for-profit corporations.

Three of these corporations were listed among the 2012 Inc 500 fastest growing small companies in America - Phone.com, DomainMarket.com and SEO.com.

Plus, he founded and operates AccurateAppraisals.com, as well as authored two books which can be found at MakeMillions.com and AppliedEvolution.com.

He also has a charitable fund, Make Change Trust! which you can read more about at MakeChange.com.

His companies exchange resources, talent and technologies in their never-ending quest to deliver innovative, profitable digital products and services.
