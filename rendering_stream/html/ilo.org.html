  • The tragic human cost of modern slavery 

    International Day for the Abolition of Slavery

    Twenty-five million people are trapped in forced labour worldwide – like the butcher portrayed in the El Patron movie by ILO advocate, Joaquin Furriel. To mark the International Day for the Abolition of Slavery, the ILO has published a toolkit to help governments design National Action Plans to combat forced labour. It is also launching guidelines on the prevention and reintegration of victims of forced labour.
