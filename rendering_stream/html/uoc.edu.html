Research and innovation

 

Research focused where technology and social & human sciences converge

The UOC has two research centres, a centre for innovation and learning transformation and a doctoral school. It also conducts research in the fields of its seven faculties.

 

Internet Interdisciplinary Institute (IN3)

Its hallmark is the interdisciplinary nature of its teams and research.

eHealth Center

Educational model

A unique model

The UOC's methodology – its proprietary learning model – is based on three basic elements: learning resources, personalized student support from teaching staff, and collaboration.
