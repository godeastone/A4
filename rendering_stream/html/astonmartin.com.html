Fuel Economy

The mpg/fuel economy figures quoted are sourced from official regulated test results obtained through laboratory testing.

They are for comparability purposes only and may not reflect your real driving experience, which may vary depending on factors including road conditions, weather, vehicle load and driving style.

Official government fuel consumption figures in mpg for the Aston Martin Vantage: City FE 14 - 18 mpg; Highway FE 21 - 24 mpg; Combined FE 17 - 20 mpg

Official government fuel consumption figures in mpg for the Aston Martin DB11: City FE 15 - 18 mpg; Highway FE 22 - 24 mpg; Combined FE 18 - 20 mpg

Official government fuel consumption figures in mpg for the Aston Martin DBS Superleggera: City FE 14 mpg; Highway FE 22 mpg; Combined FE 17 mpg

Official government fuel consumption figures in mpg for the Aston Martin Rapide AMR: City FE 18 mpg; Highway FE 24 mpg; Combined FE 20 mpg
