A Better Web Hosting Experience

  • Innovation
  • Quality
  • Helpfulness

The internet’s changed a lot since we first started hosting sites in 1998. And our teams stay active in confronting challenges and developing solutions. Grid was one of the world’s first public clouds, making promising new tech accessible to anyone. Today, we’re tackling the complexities of the AWS cloud, exploring ways to unleash its full potential for users who don’t want to master its intricacies.

Plenty of web hosts cover your basic specs as cheaply as possible and cut every corner they can. Since the beginning, we’ve put quality first – using top-tier hardware and data centers to improve the speed and reliability of your sites. Meanwhile, our engineering and user-experience teams focus on getting the most out of those physical resources. The goal is to deliver solutions that perform better than their numbers.

Problems can’t be solved by technology alone. Sometimes you need the help of another person, guiding your way or considering an issue from a different angle. Support is our great differentiator, with a team that goes the extra mile to ensure you can get the most out of your hosting solution. It’s a value that’s seen even our founder fielding support calls, and it’s why Media Temple’s reputation has grown.
