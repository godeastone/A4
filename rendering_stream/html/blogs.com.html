Frequently asked questions

What is Blogs.com by Bluehost?

Blogs.com by Bluehost is a new way to share your ideas with the world. Designed for simplicity, Blogs.com removes the complexity of traditional website hosting, and delivers a straight-forward blogging experience so that you can focus on crafting your message to your readers. This no-frills solution is perfect for anyone looking to start a blog quickly without all the extraneous tools that sometimes interfere with the creative process. Start free for 30 days, and automatically renew at $7.99/month.

What do I get with my 30 Day Free Trial?

Each account on Blogs.com comes with an automatic installation of WordPress for a single domain, as well as automatic WordPress updates. From a technical standpoint, each account comes with 50GB of storage space, free SSL certificate, and unmetered bandwidth. Blogs.com accounts are also eligible to leverage the free 30-day Blue Flash onboarding service which is designed to help new users become familiar with their dashboard and the basics of WordPress theme and plugin selection. Lastly, Blogs.com accounts are given access to Bluehost's 24/7 customer support for any assistance with troubleshooting, billing, or account questions.

How do I upgrade my account?

Blogs.com accounts can be upgraded within the dashboard to a full Bluehost Shared Hosting account. Upgrading a Blogs.com account to a full Bluehost Shared Hosting account unlocks additional tools and services within the dashboard, such as access to Microsoft Office 365, advanced cPanel controls, and more.

Can I migrate my existing blog to Blogs.com?

Yes. Once a Blogs.com account has been created, an existing blog can be migrated to the platform using either an existing 3rd party tool designed for migrating WordPress websites, or Blogs.com users can contact our support team and schedule a migration to their new account. However, migrations performed by Bluehost support will incur a one-time fee of up to $149 depending on the complexity of the migration.
