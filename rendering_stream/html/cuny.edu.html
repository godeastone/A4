Every year, hundreds of thousands of students – including a record 274,000 degree-credit students – choose The City University of New York for a multitude of reasons that can be summed up as one: opportunity. Providing a quality, accessible education, regardless of background or means, has been CUNY’s mission since 1847.

CUNY Podcasts

CUNY Is the Plot Twist in Walter Mosley’s Life

The renowned novelist found his voice–and launched his career–as a student in the graduate writing program at City College. Three decades and 60 books later, he’s the recipient of this year’s National Book Foundation lifetime achievement award. On the newest episode of the CUNYcast, Mosley talks about his life, his work and the program he created at CCNY to open doors for minorities in the publishing industry.
