Excellent research and teaching require a powerful, innovative IT infrastructure

As the university computing centre for the Georg-August-Universität Göttingen, and as a computing and IT competence centre for the Max Planck Society, we offer a wide range of information and communication services for science.

Through our own research in the field of computer science and participation in numerous research projects, we create the basis for innovative, customer-oriented IT solutions.

Our staff is available to help with all questions concerning scientific data processing, with an extensive range of advice and support services.
