A warm welcome ...

... to the official online travel guide of the city of Vienna. Here you will find all the information you need for your perfect vacation in Vienna. Due to the Covid-19 situation, cultural and leisure facilities such as museums, zoos, amusement parks, theaters, concert halls, cinemas and cabarets as well as all restaurants and hotels are currently closed. However, you will still find many great stories and tips on our website. Because one day your perfect vacation in Vienna will be possible again. Until then please stay healthy! We look forward to welcoming you back to Vienna soon.
