Stay Informed on Novel Coronavirus (COVID-19)

For accurate, up-to-date information on the virus, including prevention tips and details on Nova Scotia’s preparation, visit www.NovaScotia.ca/CoronaVirus

Province Invests $230 Million to Stimulate the Economy and Create Jobs

Government is investing up to $230 million to improve infrastructure and help get the economy moving again. The investment will create up to 2,000 local jobs as Nova Scotians get to work on more than 200 projects across the province.
