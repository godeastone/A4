On November 20, 1998 in Allston, MA, a vigil was held for Rita Hester, a Black trans woman whose candle-lit memorial became a tradition known as TDoR: a day of remembrance, resilience and resistance. On TDoR, we come together as a community to mourn and honor our friends, loved ones and community members who have died from anti-transgender violence.

"Together we take a moment to mourn, heal and imagine a new world. We ponder the imperative of gender justice. We remember that, as the Torah insists, 'Justice, Justice you shall pursue.' Here at Brandeis, the @brandeis_gsc is a resource for those who pursue justice and commit to making the world a more just and inclusive place for all." - Julián Cancino, inaugural director of the GSC

Fall is fading fast, and so is this out of the ordinary semester. Following the Thanksgiving holiday students will remain home, with all classes moving to remote learning.
