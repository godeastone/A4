While museum galleries remain temporarily closed, visit LACMA outdoors for shopping and public art.

PLEASE NOTE: Throughout the LACMA campus, visitors are required to wear a mask covering nose and mouth at all times except when eating or drinking and maintain a distance of at least 6 feet from others.

Learn more about what's open and on view. The LACMA Store inside Resnick Pavilion will also reopen.

Join us from home for virtual programs and enriching content for you to Watch, Listen, Learn, Read, and more. See below for LACMA @ Home offerings.
