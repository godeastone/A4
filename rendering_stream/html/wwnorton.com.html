    A MESSAGE TO OUR READERS

    "True community is based upon equality, mutuality, and reciprocity. It affirms the richness of individual diversity as well as the common human ties that bind us together."

    —Pauli Murray

    Literature, poetry, memoir, biography, history, composition, sociology, psychology, economics—all offer vivid revelations,
    lived experiences, and essential perspectives on the racial injustices we must fully own and
    the true community we must bravely build.

    Through reading, we seek understanding; through understanding, we seek justice. 

    As a company, we recommit ourselves to this crucial work.  

    Black stories matter. Black Lives Matter.
