10,000

If a petition gets 10,000 signatures, the government will respond

  1. Implement a two week school lockdown before 24 December to save Christmas

    The government responded on 1 December 2020

    As set out in the Government’s COVID-19 Winter Plan, schools should not close early or change their Christmas holidays. It remains a national priority to avoid further disruption to education.

    Read the response to ‘Implement a two week school lockdown before 24 December to save Christmas’ in full

  2. End lockdown to prevent further economic damage to the UK’s economy.

    The government responded on 1 December 2020

    The Government has announced the Covid 19 Winter Plan, which sets out how we will continue to support the economy whilst limiting the spread of the virus.

    Read the response to ‘End lockdown to prevent further economic damage to the UK’s economy.’ in full

  3. Do not implement a second national lockdown or other restrictions this winter

    The government responded on 30 November 2020

    The Government introduced new measures in England on 5 November to protect the NHS and reduce the growth in hospitalisations and deaths. Similar steps were taken in other parts of the UK.

    Read the response to ‘Do not implement a second national lockdown or other restrictions this winter’ in full
