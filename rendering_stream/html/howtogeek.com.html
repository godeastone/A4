Did You Know?

Scenes in the movie The Matrix are tinted based on where they take place. Scenes within the Matrix itself have a green tint (to represent the green monochromatic screens of early computers), scenes that occur in real life outside the Matrix have a blue tint, and the fight training scene between Morpheus and Neo (neither inside nor outside the Matrix) has a yellow tint.
