Our UK campuses



Our campuses in Edinburgh, Scottish Borders and Orkney each offer bespoke facilities and a distinctive learning environment.









Top 60





We are ranked in the top 60 of Golden Age Universities in the world.

THE Golden Age University Rankings 2018



















Edinburgh


One of the world’s most beautiful cities with an exceptional quality of life, Edinburgh is Scotland’s capital and a great place to live and study.














Scottish Borders


Perfectly situated at the pulsating heart of Scotland's luxury textile industry, our Borders campus delivers the best design and production facilities for textiles and fashion in the UK.














Orkney


Orkney's stunning setting, extensive natural resources, ties to the marine industry and friendly community make a distinctive learning environment.
