Spotlighting IUPUI

Black Lives Matter mural makes its debut along Indiana Avenue

Diversity, equity, and inclusion

A Black Lives Matter mural, created by local Indianapolis artists, was unveiled on August 1st. Its location on the north side of campus on Indiana Avenue, between Blackford Street and Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. Street, near the Madame Walker Legacy Center and the Indianapolis Urban League building, was chosen for its historical significance to the Black community.

SHHS makes legacy gift to campus with new Fitness garden

Campus update

The new 5,095 square foot fitness garden, gifted to IUPUI from the School of Health and Human Sciences, is an easily accessible outdoor gym located just outside the south entrance of the Natatorium. The space features 33 fitness stations, tables and benches with charging stations for outdoor classrooms.
