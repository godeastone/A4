NEWS & ANNOUNCEMENTS

Spring 2021 semester information

Building on what we’ve achieved together this fall semester, the spring 2021 calendar has been modified in response to the ongoing pandemic. Key changes to our spring schedule of classes are as follows: spring semester classes will begin on Monday, Jan. 25, later than originally scheduled; spring break will not be held; and final exams will take place Monday, May 3 through Friday, May 7.

UNI's speech language pathology department first in the nation to use the Paul Simulator

Thanks to a $85,000 grant from the Roy J. Carver Charitable Trust Fund, the UNI Department of Communication Sciences and Disorders is now the first speech-language pathology department in the nation to use the Paul Simulator, which is a top-of-the-line high emotion simulator that students in the department will soon begin using for classwork.
