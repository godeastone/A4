University recognised by THE for social responsibility work

Turing AI Fellow to deliver research into cancer evolution

Professor Christopher Yau has received a Turing Artificial Intelligence (AI) Acceleration Fellowship to develop AI-driven predictive models to describe how cancers evolve.

University commits to 100% renewable energy from 2021

This major milestone is part of Manchester’s plan to move towards sustainable energy consumption and completely decarbonise campus.

Further rent reduction and action on student accommodation

The University, working with the Students’ Union, has agreed to give all students in University halls of residences a reduction for semester 1 totalling 30%.

Academic recognised as one of the world's most cited scholars

Frank Geels, Professor of System Innovation and Sustainability, came 129th in an analysis of the world's top-cited scholars by Stanford Meta-Research Centre.

Professor Gary Younge to deliver public lecture on US election

Gary Younge, award-winning journalist and Professor of Sociology at Manchester, will deliver the live-streamed lecture on Thursday, 19 November.

Manchester secures £1.5 million funding to revolutionise plastic waste streams

The ‘one bin to rule them all’ project aims to simplify plastic recycling for consumers so that more usable recycled plastics can be fed back into a circular economy.
