After 15 years of blogging, Design*Sponge has officially closed its doors. From the bottom of our hearts, thank you for sharing this online home with us.

For FAQs and more on our closing, please visit our final post here.

Design*Sponge will remain online through September of 2020, thanks to generous support from Adam J. Kurtz and Tuesday Bassen.

We’re thrilled to announce that our full archives will be available online (and fully searchable) through the Library of Congress, starting in September of 2020. We will provide an updated link to those archives once they are open to the public.
