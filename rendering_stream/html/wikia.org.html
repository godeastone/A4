What is Wikia.org?
Wikia.org shines a light on some of the world's most important topics, creating a platform for passionate people to share information about history, social causes, politics, and more.
History
From more recent events to the far-flung age of the dinosaurs, Wikia.org documents the many facets of world history.
Advocacy
Our moment in history demands that people come together to help one another. Wikia.org is a resource for important social causes around diversity and inclusion, suicide prevention, and homelessness.
Politics and religion
Wikia.org provides an in-depth look at political causes, the history and civics of local cities and towns, and the world religions.
