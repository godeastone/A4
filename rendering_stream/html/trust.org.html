We are the corporate foundation of Thomson Reuters, the global news and information services company. We work to advance media freedom, foster more inclusive economies, and promote human rights.

Our Approach

Through news, media development, free legal assistance, training and convening initiatives, we combine our unique services to drive systemic change.

Our commitment to the fight against racism and discrimination

The Thomson Reuters Foundation stands with the Black community today and every day: Racism, bigotry and prejudice have no place in society. We will harness the power of our media and legal services to play a more proactive role in the continued fight for equality and more inclusive economies.
