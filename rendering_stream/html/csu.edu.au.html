                          Bloom 2020 Creative Industries showcase

                          Date

                          16 Oct 2020 9:00 am - 16 Oct 2021 9:00 am

                          BLOOM 2020 is the end of year festival for students studying the Creative Industries in the School of Communication and Creative Industries.

                          This new initiative celebrates the most ambitious and exciting work produced by Creative Industries students of all disciplines throughout the year. The festival will include outcomes by actors, animators, artists, art historians, curators, designers, film makers, photographers, sound and television producers and theatre-makers.

                          It is a multi-disciplinary festival showcasing the very best our school has to offer. Bloom launches at bloom.csu.edu.au on October 16th.

                          For more, please visit https://about.csu.edu.au/community/events/upcoming/dubbo/bloom-2020-creative-industries-showcase

                          Virtual Run Event #runCharlesSturtrun

                          Date

                          1 Dec 2020 6:00 am - 31 Dec 2020 6:00 pm

                          Get active online and get talking about mental health

                          #runCharlesSturtrun

                          We’re inviting our alumni community to get active with us and get talking about mental health!

                          We’ve organised a virtual fun run for our alumni and all you need to do to get involved is complete a 5-kilometre run, walk or cycle anytime in the month of December and share your results on social media using the hashtag #runCharlesSturtrun.

                          Register to get involved

                          Register Now and you’ll receive

                          • A Charles Sturt cap
                          • Bonus training t-shirt for the first 70 people to register
                          • Training tips and tricks from fellow alumni

                          The entry fee is $10 which we’ll donate on your behalf to our Alumni give scholarship fund to support students in need. It’s been a difficult year with more students than ever facing financial hardship, social isolation and associated mental health issues. Your participation could make a huge difference!

                          Got a question?

                          If you need any more information please email us at alumni@csu.edu.au

                          Venue / Address:

                          For more, please visit https://alumni.csu.edu.au/news-and-events/events/community/virtual-run-event-runcharlessturtrun
