Find your Edge at UVic

Learning

At UVic, every student is immersed in dynamic learning that’s fuelled by research-inspired insights and personalized, hands-on experiences that bring learning out of the classroom and into the world.

Impact

As an internationally renowned teaching and research hub, we tackle essential issues that matter to our world and our communities, improving lives and making a vital impact across the globe.

Environment

Located on the rugged west coast, we emphasize human connection on a global scale, with teaching and research intertwined in our extraordinary academic environment.

You'll be immersed in dynamic, hands-on learning; research that makes a vital impact around the world; and discovery and innovation in Canada’s most extraordinary academic environment.
