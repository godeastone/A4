FALL 2020 GRAD CAP CONTEST

We want to see our graduating Mavericks' decorated caps! All eligible entries sent to socialmedia@uta.edu will be uploaded to a Fall 2020 #FOREVERMAVERICK album on the UTA Facebook page after the entry period closes on December 17. Voting will commence on December 18.

See Full Contest Rules

Fall 2020 Virtual Celebration

While we await the postponed commencement ceremonies, we invite graduates, families, friends, alumni and the entire UTA Community to celebrate on December 17 at 7:30 p.m. Immediately after the virtual celebration, you are cordially invited to a Post Toast Graduation Celebration hosted by the UTA Parent & Family Center.
