Spreaker

Home of the endless podcast possibilities

Spreaker is the one-stop shop for podcast hosting, creation, distribution and monetization. Producing your passion project or growing a podcast business has never been easier.

Start podcasting

Podcast Hosting Made Easy with Spreaker

Creating impactful content is effortless with Spreaker. For more than a decade, our team and product has been equipping podcasters and publishers with all the tools they need to succeed.

Enterprise

The ultimate enterprise podcast platform

We’re the unrivaled podtech solution for publishers and large distributed teams to effortlessly manage their podcasts. Our tools for podcast publishers include everything needed to grow, while providing you with options to customize some features in order to meet your needs.

Explore enterprise capabilities

Publish

Simplified Podcast Publishing

Once your podcast is created, publishing your episode is a breeze. With Spreaker’s desktop Content Management System (CMS), you can publish your podcast in a matter of seconds. There’s no waiting… your content can be heard instantly.

Start Publishing

Distribute

Streamlined Podcast Distribution

With the click of one button, you can distribute your episodes to all the top podcast platforms and directories like Apple Podcasts, Spotify, Google Podcasts, and more

Start Distributing

Monetize

Effortless Podcast Monetization

The ability to monetize podcasts is no longer reserved for the elite! You can earn money with every listen with our monetization program. In one simple click, you become a part of our programmatic advertising system, which automatically inserts ads into your podcast.

Monetize your podcasts

Record

Podcast on the Go with Spreaker Studio

If you’re looking to create a podcast, Spreaker Studio is the fastest way to publish the podcasts you’re producing. If you’re ready to create and record a podcast now, our software is available for download as an app, which makes the lives of our podcasters even easier.

Discover Spreaker Studio
