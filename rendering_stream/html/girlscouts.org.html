    Our Stories

    The power of the Girl Scout Experience

    Kennedy: Empowering Through Civic Leadership

    I built a database of more than 50 partners offering service learning opportunities for students at my school.

    • Kiara: Increasing Access to Local Fresh Food

      I created the Very Hungry Caterpillar Garden in my community—to grow healthy fruits and vegetables that would be distributed through the local food pantry.

    • Julia: Fighting Menstrual Stigma

      I reached out to my state representative, who agreed to sponsor a bill that would fund menstrual products in Colorado schools.

    • Alex: Putting the Brakes on Distracted Driving

      I made five videos to explain distracted driving, created a petition to make Alabama a “hands free” state.

    • Natalie: Designing a Helipad that Saves Lives

      Through Girl Scouts, I was able to learn about robotics in an environment where I felt safe and validated.

    Whether they are protecting our environment, lobbying the city council, or speaking up for the vulnerable, Girl Scouts always have unforgettable, inspiring stories to tell about how they make a difference in the world.
