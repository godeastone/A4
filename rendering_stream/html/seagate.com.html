Democratizing Hyperscale Storage Architecture

Most enterprise data centers are not designed for the mass capacity imperative of today’s data economy. With a tightly-integrated roadmap, Seagate brings hyperscale cost and architectural efficiencies to the enterprise storage stack across software, silicon, system and device innovations. Accelerate adoption of these leading storage technologies as quickly as they’re available, and give your data center a competitive edge.

Capacity
Increased Areal Density

Industry-leading cost per TB with unmatched hard drive capacity at 18TB. Next generation 20TB HAMR (Heat Assisted Magnetic Recording) drives shipping in 2020 with a roadmap through to 50TB.

Performance
Multi-actuator

Multi-actuator (Mach.2) enabled parallelism doubles hard drive performance, maintaining performance SLA’s without sacrificing capacity.

Scalability
CORTX: Intelligent Object Storage Software

100% open source, exabyte-scale object storage software enabling reliability, mass capacity, and architectural efficiency.

Resilience

ADAPT Erasure Coding

High-capacity data protection that replaces traditional RAID types and enables rebuilds up to 95% faster with no compromise on capacity.
