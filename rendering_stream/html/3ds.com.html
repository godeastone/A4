3DXCITE Engineer the Excitement 3DXCITE® software, solutions, and CGI services provide high-end 3D visualizations in real-time for high-impact storytelling across all media channels. More on 3DXCITE
CENTRICPLM Centric Software’s Innovations Meet Centric’s market driven solutions that are fast to deploy, easy to use and designed to drive business growth. More on CENTRICPLM
ENOVIA Plan Your Definition of Success ENOVIA® delivers new levels of 3D digital collaboration including PDM, design data management and (BOM) Bill of materials softwares in companies large and small. ENOVIA allows companies to fully master the creation and lifecycle management of products whether they are complex or simple. More on ENOVIA
SOLIDWORKS Authentic Design Experience SOLIDWORKS® leads the global 3D computer-aided design (CAD) industry with easy-to-use 3D software that trains and supports the world's engineering and design teams as they drive tomorrow's product innovation. More on SOLIDWORKS
CATIA Shape The World We Live In CATIA® is the world's engineering and design leading software for product 3D CAD design excellence. It addresses all manufacturing organizations, from OEMs through their supply chains, to small independent producers. More on CATIA
GEOVIA Model the Planet GEOVIA® is a world-leading solution for modeling and simulating our Planet to improve predictability, efficiency, safety and sustainability of our natural resources. More on GEOVIA
BIOVIA Model the Biosphere BIOVIA® provides a collaborative scientific enterprise environment for modeling, simulation, laboratory, and quality management for advanced biological, chemical, and materials innovation. More on BIOVIA
SIMULIA Reveal The World We Live In SIMULIA®, is defining new standards to establish Finite Element Analysis and realistic simulation softwares as an integral business process in the engineering value chain. More on SIMULIA
DELMIA Make it happen DELMIA® is the modeling key software for delivering a digital manufacturing process environment to optimize production systems and processes. More on DELMIA
3DVIA 3D by Me 3DVIA® apps create that magical moment, when people immediately see and understand what you mean. For people in sales, product marketing, training, and support, 3DVIA creates engaging and intuitive 3D interactive apps that advance communications and accelerate learning. More on 3DVIA
EXALEAD Reveal Information Intelligence EXALEAD® cognitive applications transform large volumes of heterogeneous, multi-source data into meaningful, real-time information intelligence to help users improve business processes and gain competitive advantage. More on EXALEAD
NETVIBES See What's Happening NETVIBES offers an easy and fast way to create personalized dashboards for real-time monitoring, social analytics, knowledge sharing, and decision support. More on NETVIBES
MEDIDATA Power smarter treatments and healthier people MEDIDATA generates the evidence and insights to help pharmaceutical, biotech, medical device and diagnostics companies, and academic researchers accelerate value, minimize risk, and optimize outcomes. More on MEDIDATA
