  • View the Interactive Map Get Directions Take a Virtual Tour of IUP

    Indiana University of Pennsylvania

    1. Oak Grove

      Hi! I'm Zeke. Thanks for joining me on this tour of IUP. Let's start here in the Oak Grove, which is probably the most popular place on campus. It's the heart of IUP, because so much happens here. Just look around and see. If you know anyone who has gone to IUP, you can bet they spent some time in the Oak Grove, and they wish they could come back. Many of them do come back.

      Before we get started, let me explain the many ways you can explore our beautiful space.

      -You can click on the forward-facing arrow to start walking around.
      -You can jump to the next stop by clicking on the "Next" button.
      -Now, if you already know where you want to go, you can select any location by using the list OR by clicking directly on the map.
      -Lastly, remember that you can explore any particular location in more detail by clicking the supplemental icons.

    2. Humanities and Social Sciences Building

      OK, let's talk about classes. More specifically, where you'll take them. Across IUP, you'll find locations like this one, which is the Humanities and Social Sciences Building. Our buildings have everything you need to be successful. More important, though, are the professors who not only teach you but also learn right beside you. Our programs are spread out over six colleges, and we know you'll find one that will take you places.

    3. North Dining

      You've got to eat, right? At our many dining spots, you don't have to settle for boring. North Dining opened in 2017, and it has everything you could possibly want. Sushi? We got it. Pizza and Pasta? Yep, those too. We also have some names you know, like Chick-fil-A, Einstein Brothers, Subway, and Starbucks. Oh, and don't forget to get some gelato here at North Dining.

    4. Northern Suites

      At IUP, where you live is just as important as where you learn. You need to be comfortable. You need to feel safe. IUP has eight residence halls, and you'll find everything you need to succeed and have fun. Something extra: You can be part of a living-learning community, where students with interests like yours live and study together. Some of the best friendships a lot of students have made happened right here. This is just another way IUP can transform your life. Are you wondering what's in that big glass case? It's a Triceratops skeleton. This is Northern Suites, where science students live.

    5. The HUB

      Hey, if you're going to work hard, you should play hard, too. This is the HUB, where you can find things to do to help you take a break. You can play basketball here or blow off some steam on a treadmill. Run around the indoor track or lift some weights. The HUB is also a good spot to see concerts and other shows, attend conferences and hang out with your friends.

    6. Downtown Indiana

      Take a walk up here and you'll see it for yourself. There's a lot to do here in downtown Indiana and it's not far from campus. It's where a lot of good restaurants and coffee shops are, not to mention some stores that have have a lot of different things. Like the Oak Grove is the heart of the campus, Philadelphia Street is the heart of our town. Check it out.

    7. University Libraries

      You already know what a library is, but ours offers more than you might expect. First, this is a living, breathing help center. If you need a hand with any of your classwork, you can get it here. It's also the home of the University College, where our explorers begin their college careers. You'll find space to work with a group or on your own. You can do research on any of the four floors, or do it online. It's just a great place to get some work done. Oh, and you can get some fuel at Java City, the coffee shop on the first floor.

    8. Eberly College of Business and Information Technology

      IUP is in a small town, but people feel our impact across the globe. One of the places you see proof of that is right here in the Eberly College of Business and Information Technology. What you do at IUP will impact your life and the lives of people thousands of miles away, in other cultures, other lands, places with different languages and different histories. You're not just learning by the book. We use technology to reach people around the world. It's part of the mission at IUP: to impact the world one person at a time.

    9. Kovalchick Convention and Athletic Complex

      Everyone at IUP is part of the Crimson Hawks family, and the Kovalchick Complex is one of the places we get together to show our pride. Our basketball and volleyball teams play here and we cheer on football, lacrosse and field hockey next door at Miller Stadium. Kovalchick is also the site of concerts and events like IUP Day for our student organizations, career fairs, commencement, Cirque shows, and more. When one of us wins, we all win. You should join us.

    10. Sutton Hall

      Let's wrap it up here. This is Sutton Hall, IUP's first building when it opened in 1875. It's more than offices and people. There's a lot of history here. It looks a little bit different than it did back then, but what happens here hasn't changed because IUP's mission hasn't. The people and opportunities at IUP will help you grow--and then you'll go on to change the world. If you have any questions about IUP, come to Sutton Hall and visit our Admissions Office. They can help.
