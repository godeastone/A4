Pre-travel testing

Some of your favorite destinations, including Hawaii, Alaska, Chicago, and New York, require negative Covid-19 test results taken no more than 72 hours before your flight, or you'll need to comply with a 14-day self-quarantine. Be sure to schedule your test early, as appointments fill up in advance.
