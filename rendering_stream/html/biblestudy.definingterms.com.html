This is the website for a University of Michigan GCF small group Bible study that met from May of 2006 through March of 2009. The site is maintained both to keep a historical record of the study and also in the hope that someone else might find the information useful. Feel free to use the syllabi and other resources on the site if you find them helpful or informative.
