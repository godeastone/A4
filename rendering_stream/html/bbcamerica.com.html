Based on the 2013 novel by Joe Hill, 'NOS4A2' introduces Vic McQueen, a gifted young woman who discovers she has a supernatural ability to find lost things. Vic strives to defeat Manx and rescue his victims — without losing her mind or falling victim to him herself.
