We simply do not believe that it should cost thousands of dollars to create a will, form a business, or apply for a trademark. So we started a movement to make legal help available to all.

Legal built for you

No complicated forms. No robots. Just answer some questions and we'll take care of the paperwork for you.

Attorneys in all 50 states

No matter where you are in the country, an independent attorney who knows your state's laws is here to help.

Flat-fee pricing

Whether you use a self-guided or attorney-supported service, you can always count us to be upfront about pricing.

Where does your life meet legal?

Let's talk about your life so the legal stuff doesn't seem so...legal. Then we can connect you with the help you need!

At home

Your spouse, kids, home, pets, and valuables live under your protection. Estate planning, despite its name, isn't just for people with mansions. Our vetted network of independent attorneys know the ins and outs of wills and trusts and are here to guide you.

At work

Your business structure, where you work, legal forms, business name and logo-there are countless work-related things that intersect with the law. Our network of attorneys are here to help you thrive.

In your creative space

Your ideas, brand names, writings, music and inventions should be…yours. We'll help you protect them so you don't have to worry about others using your work.
