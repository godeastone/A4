Fotolia is now closed, learn more about Adobe Stock and the Fotolia Closure

Why has Fotolia closed?

After fourteen years in business, Fotolia has transitioned its members to Adobe Stock on a voluntary basis in order to offer them a better and more streamlined service as well as deeper integration within Adobe Creative Cloud applications.

What is the Transfer and Migration offer?

The Adobe Stock Transfer and Migration Offer gives eligible Fotolia members the opportunity to convert their unused Fotolia credits to Adobe Stock credits. Additionally, your Fotolia license history, lightboxes, galleries, and other account information will transfer to Adobe Stock account.

Customers who need more robust features and grants of rights should consider an Adobe Stock enterprise plan. Your Fotolia license history will still be transferred to your new Adobe Stock enterprise account with the additional benefits of the enterprise admin console. Please contact your local account manager to learn more. If you are unsure of who your local account manager may be, the Fotolia customer service team will be happy to connect you.
