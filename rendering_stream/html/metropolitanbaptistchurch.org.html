Metropolitan Baptist Church is a friendly Bible believing church located in Sandy Springs, GA., dedicated to reaching the heart and home for Jesus Christ! We welcome families, couples and singles to worship with us. We sing the old hymns of the faith; our preaching is based upon the Word of God, and our message is Christ crucified, risen and coming again (1 Corinthians 1:18-25).
