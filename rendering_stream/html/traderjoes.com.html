Bake It 'Til You Make It

Holiday baking is akin to an Olympic sport: the recipe research, numerous test batches to get the ratio of dough to mix-ins just right, and of course the endless eating of treats...it’s a task we take seriously, so our customers can enjoy themselves in the kitchen with our tried & true baking mixes. Here we've gathered our newest crop of holiday baking mix contestants, plus a recipe if you want to go the extra mile and experiment with from-scratch preparation. Head to your neighborhood Trader Joe's, grab a mix (or grab them all), and bake your way to the finish line of 2020.
