County Facts

Population: 469,966

History: Named for Ada Riggs, daughter of H.C. Riggs, one of the founders of Boise and a member of the Idaho Territorial Legislature who introduced legislation to move the capital from Lewiston to Boise. Boise became the capital of Idaho in 1865.

Vehicle License Designator: 1A
