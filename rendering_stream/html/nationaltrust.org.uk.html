Ideas for days out in our gardens and parks

What's open?

We're open and safe for you to visit. Many gardens, parks, cafés, coast and countryside sites and some houses are open for you to explore. When planning your visit, please follow government guidance and your local tier restrictions.

To avoid disappointment please book your visit in advance, especially at weekends and school holidays. Every Friday we release new tickets for the following week. Please always check the property webpage of the place you wish to visit before you travel.

What's closed?

In tier 3 areas in England, houses and other indoor areas remain closed and cafés are takeaway only.

In line with Welsh government legislation, National Trust houses in Wales will close from 5 December.

In Northern Ireland, all houses, shops and visitor centres are closed until 11 December following the Northern Ireland Executive circuit break restrictions. Cafés remain takeaway only.
