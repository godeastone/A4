Ladders: 9 outdated business attire clothing items that age you

AOM research shows that people who are considered “highly attractive” earn an average of 20% more than their less attractive counterparts. Wearing clothing that makes you look older than you are could land you firmly in the “less attractive” category by masking your youthful vigor and casting your stellar work ethic into the shadows.
