    About

    The University of Massachusetts Medical School (UMMS) is the commonwealth’s first and only public academic health sciences center, home to three graduate schools. Our mission is to advance the health and wellness of our diverse communities throughout Massachusetts and across the world by leading and innovating in education, research, health care delivery and public service.

    Research

    The University of Massachusetts Medical School (UMMS) is a world-class research institution with a uniquely collaborative spirit, that consistently produces noteworthy advances in clinical and basic research. With $257 million (FY18) in research funding, UMMS is in the top two percent of all NIH-funded institutions.

Commonwealth Medicine is dedicated to improving health care for people in need, while also controlling costs and maximizing revenue for our clients. Public- and private-sector organizations save millions of dollars annually by working with us. Our evidence-based recommendations and innovative solutions produce tangible results:

  • Better health outcomes
  • Lower health care costs
  • More access to health care
