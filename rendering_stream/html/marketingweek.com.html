Xeim offers consultancy, capability and advisory services. Oystercatchers help brands meet, select and work with the best creative agencies; as well as helping marketing leaders shape their organisational design to drive growth. Econsultancy offers consultancy and capability services to develop the knowledge, skills and mindset of every marketing, digital and ecommerce professional, globally.

 

At Xeim our internationally acclaimed media and information brands including Marketing Week, Econsultancy, Creative Review and Influencer Intelligence provide inspiration, insight, analysis and digital training to help clients deliver marketing excellence.With a choice of in-company or blended learning solutions, Econsultancy and Marketing Week offer a range of training including marketing strategy, digital transformation and ecommerce.

 

Xeim brings our partners and marketers together across our leading media brands, such as Marketing Week and Creative Review, and across a series of conferences, awards and large-scale events. Our events business is spear-headed by the Festival of Marketing in London, the world’s largest dedicated event for marketers. Our B2B marketing services operations – Marketmakers and Really specialise in data services, content marketing solutions, account-based marketing and telemarketing to deliver leads and opportunities for businesses that want to connect with each other.
