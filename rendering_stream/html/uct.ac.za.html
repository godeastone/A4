Please note that undergraduate applications to UCT have closed on 31 August 2020. Undergraduate applicants who need financial assistance must submit separate applications to NSFAS by 30 November 2020.

Notice of the Convocation AGM 2020
Members of UCT Convocation are hereby notified that the 2020 Annual General Meeting (AGM) of the Convocation will be convened on a virtual online platform, at 17:00 on Wednesday, 9 December 2020. In order to facilitate online access to the 2020 AGM of the Convocation meeting, members will be required to register by no later than Wednesday, 18 November 2020.
