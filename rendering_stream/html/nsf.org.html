What We Do

Testing, Inspection and Certification

We provide certification, testing and auditing to public health standards in all key industries and sectors worldwide.

Consulting

Separate from certification, our experts provide world-class strategic and technical consulting services tailored to you.

Training and Education

Our online, on-site and on-demand training keeps companies ahead of the latest regulatory requirements and industry best practices.

Standards Development

For over 75 years, NSF International has led the development of more than 100 American National Standards for public health, environment and sustainability assessment.

What Does the NSF Mark Mean?

The NSF mark is your assurance that a product has been tested by a trusted independent certification organization. It is valued by consumers, manufacturers, retailers and regulatory agencies worldwide.
