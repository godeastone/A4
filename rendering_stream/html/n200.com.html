Visit Connect

Welcome to Visit

Our smart exhibition technology takes digital tools and applies them to the exhibition floor. 

For many years, we have offered measurement and lead generation tools to organisers and their exhibitors, now, we are first to market with the tools to enable organisers to empower their visitors to take control of their experience at an event.

See Visit technology in action.

Our smart exhibition model is driven by visitor and exhibitor interaction to ensure every opportunity is maximised.

Effortless for Visitors

We empower the visitor to take greater control of their visiting experience. Our smart badge holds all the technology a visitor needs to navigate a show. It serves as a digital briefcase to an exhibition, enabling richer interaction with exhibitors, content, sessions and features, all via a simple touch.

360° Lead Capture for Exhibitors

Exhibitors now have the benefit of two forms of lead capture to ensure no leads are missed; Visit Connect for detailed, qualified lead capture and Visit Touchpoints, which enable a visitor with a smart badge to collect content and exchange contact details.
